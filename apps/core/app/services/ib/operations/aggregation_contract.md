# IB Operations Aggregation Contract

This contract is the Phase 7 coordinator intelligence backbone.

## Inputs
- curriculum documents
- evidence items
- learning stories and publishing queue state
- specialist collaborator assignments
- operational records
- standards packets
- PYP POI entries
- workflow/activity telemetry

## Outputs
- summary metrics for home and operations surfaces
- exception queues with deterministic routes
- programme-specific intelligence overlays
- recommendation candidates
- export/share snapshots
- release-gate and readiness evidence

## Guardrails
- tenant and school scope are applied before aggregation
- no guardian-only narrative text is copied into coordinator telemetry
- every exception row must carry a deterministic route
- derived metrics must be explainable from stored records, not opaque AI scoring
