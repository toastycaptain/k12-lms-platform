# IB Phase 4 Release Gates

## MYP gate

- Create and open MYP unit, interdisciplinary, project, and service routes from programme-native entry points.
- Confirm concept/context, inquiry, criteria, and ATL summaries render from live unit data.
- Confirm project and service checkpoint updates persist and re-render.
- Confirm coverage and review routes deep-link to live units and records.
- Confirm student and guardian surfaces show MYP next-action and milestone summaries without internal-only detail.

## DP gate

- Create and open DP course maps on canonical `/ib/dp/course-maps/*` routes.
- Launch IA, EE, TOK, and CAS records directly from a course map.
- Confirm checkpoint updates persist on IA/EE/TOK/CAS detail routes.
- Confirm coordinator route shows live risk plus student overview drilldowns.
- Confirm student and guardian surfaces show DP next actions and support prompts without coordinator-only detail.

## No-regression gate

- Route registry resolves all canonical phase 4 pages and legacy aliases.
- Shared record-workspace interactions emit telemetry.
- PYP routes remain unchanged and continue to resolve.
